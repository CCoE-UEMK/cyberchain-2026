What is eHaCON 2026?

The 8th International Ethical Hacking Conference (eHaCON 2026 – 2nd Edition), themed “Cyberchain: Securing the Future with Blockchain, AI, and Cyber Resilience”, brings together leading academicians, researchers, industry practitioners, policymakers, and innovators to discuss cutting-edge developments in ethical hacking, cybersecurity, blockchain technologies, artificial intelligence, and emerging digital systems.

The conference aims to promote high-quality research, foster interdisciplinary knowledge exchange, and strengthen global collaboration toward building secure, trustworthy, and resilient digital infrastructures.
