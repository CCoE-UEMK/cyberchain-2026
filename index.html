Welcome to CYBERCHAIN 2026

CYBERCHAIN 2026 is the premier international conference organized by the Cybersecurity Centre of Excellence (CCoE), in association with the Department of Computer Applications, University of Engineering & Management (UEM), Kolkata.

The conference aims to bring researchers, academicians, industry experts, and practitioners together to discuss innovations and breakthroughs in Blockchain, Cybersecurity, Artificial Intelligence, and Emerging Technologies.
