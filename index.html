Welcome to eHaCON 2026 : Cyberchain

The 8th International Ethical Hacking Conference (eHaCON 2026 – 2nd Edition), themed “Cyberchain: Securing the Future with Blockchain, AI, and Cyber Resilience”, is organized by the Cybersecurity Centre of Excellence (CCoE), in association with the Department of Computer Applications, University of Engineering & Management (UEM), Kolkata.

The conference aims to bring together researchers, academicians, industry experts, and practitioners to discuss innovations, challenges, and breakthroughs in Blockchain technologies, Cybersecurity, Artificial Intelligence, and next-generation digital systems.

Conference Details

Date: 11–12 December 2026

Time: 09:00 AM – 05:30 PM IST (GMT+5:30)

Mode: Hybrid

Venue: University of Engineering and Management, Kolkata

About the Conference

eHaCON 2026 : Cyberchain provides a global platform for presenting cutting-edge research, exchanging ideas, and fostering collaborations in ethical hacking, blockchain systems, cryptographic security, cyber defense, secure AI models, and disruptive digital technologies shaping future ecosystems.
